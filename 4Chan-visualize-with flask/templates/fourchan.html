4Chan Analysis

4Chan is an online platform where anyone can share images and post their comments. For 4Chan, users need not register to post their content and thereby featuring anonymity and is widely used for memes, slangs. Due to the huge number of users posting their opinion and free comments on a topic, 4Chan is a platform where analysis on election can be done with the extensive amount of data.

We have done analysis of the data collected from 4Chan posts to discover which party has more positive feedback among the voters and here we will be visualizing these data. We found that democrats had a higher positive response but why Republicans won despite this output?
